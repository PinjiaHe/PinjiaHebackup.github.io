General

Pinjia is a postdoctoral scholar at ETH Zurich, working with Prof. Zhendong Su. He received his Ph.D. from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2018, supervised by Prof. Michael R. Lyu.

Our project LogPAI (1000+ stars in total) aims to provide an end-to-end solution to intelligently manage and analyze logs for modern production systems.

Research Interests

Robust intelligent software; AIOps; Log management; Data-driven software engineering.
