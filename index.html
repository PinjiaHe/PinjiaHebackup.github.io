General

This is Pinjia He, a postdoctoral scholar in the Advanced Software Technologies (AST) Lab at ETH Zurich, mentored by Prof. Zhendong Su. I received my Ph.D. from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2018, supervised by Prof. Michael R. Lyu. I received a B.E. from South China University of Technology.

I am mainly known for my research on testing AI software and AIOps. My students and I develop tools and methodologies that enhance the reliability of both traditional software and AI software. My research spans software engineering, natural language processing, deep learning, and blockchain.

I will be joining The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhenas an assistant professor this Fall. Our group has open positions for PhD students, postdocs, research assistants, and visitors. If you are interested in our group, please drop me an email.
